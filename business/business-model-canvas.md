## Customer Segments
- **Open‑source project maintainers** (core maintainers of libraries, frameworks, and tools) – ~150 k active maintainers on GitHub.
- **Contributor communities** (volunteer developers, students, and hobbyists) – ~2 M monthly active contributors.
- **Enterprise teams that rely on open‑source** (DevOps, SRE, security groups) – ~30 k teams needing governance over upstream projects.
- **Non‑profit foundations** that sponsor open‑source (e.g., CNCF, Apache) – 50 + organizations seeking sustainability metrics.

## Value Propositions
- **Scope‑visibility dashboard** – real‑time view of open issues, PRs, and feature requests with weighted “scope‑risk” scores to flag potential creep.
- **Burnout‑prevention metrics** – automated workload heat‑maps per maintainer, with alerts when weekly contribution hours exceed safe thresholds.
- **Prioritization engine** – AI‑driven ranking of tasks based on impact, community demand, and security urgency.
- **Governance templates** – ready‑to‑use project charter, contribution guidelines, and release‑policy docs that embed scope‑control checkpoints.
- **Open‑source‑friendly pricing** – free core, paid “Pro” add‑ons for advanced analytics and enterprise SSO integration.

## Channels
- **GitHub Marketplace** listing (primary acquisition point for maintainers).
- **Community webinars & hackathons** (partnered with CNCF, OpenJS Foundation).
- **Developer newsletters & Reddit/Discord communities** (r/opensource, r/devops).
- **Direct sales outreach** to enterprise DevOps leads (LinkedIn, email campaigns).
- **Documentation site & open‑source repo** (self‑service onboarding, free trial).

## Customer Relationships
- **Self‑service onboarding** with guided walkthroughs and video tutorials.
- **Community support** via public issue tracker, Slack/Discord channel, and monthly AMA sessions.
- **Dedicated success managers** for Enterprise Pro customers (quarterly health checks, custom reporting).
- **Feedback loops** – in‑app surveys and a public roadmap to co‑create features.
- **Open‑source contribution program** – maintainers can earn “Guard Champion” badges for helping improve the tool.
